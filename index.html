      ERP SYSTEM

      ERP system is a multi-tenant system that provides a full range of key modules to support business operations, especially for IT outsourcing companies. ERP system includes financial management, human resource management, production management, procurement management, sales management, talent management, customer care management, project management, checkpoint procedure, etc. In addition to the main modules, the ERP also provides users with custom modules. Therefore, business owners can be assured that business management software fully meets the needs of their business. ...

      ERP is integrated with the email system and Komu, the custom internal communication system, to maximize synchronicity and security. Especially, Komu has many utilities such as meeting rooms, entertaining rooms, notification, working time management, personnel status checking, etc which makes it become an ideal solution for the remote working era.
